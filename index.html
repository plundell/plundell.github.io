Paragast WebApp

What is a webapp?

Unlike a native mobile app which you download from the app store, a webapp is just a shortcut on your homescreen to a webpage which runs in your browser. However, thanks to modern web technologies these now work offline, they can check for new messages/content in the background and notify you even when your browser isn't open - just like a native apps!

How to install:

  1. Allow Paragast to send notifications
  2. Download and create shortcut on homescreen
